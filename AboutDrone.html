About Our Drones

Our drone was selected by a group of exprets which would allow us to maximize area coverage and efficency. We will do this by employing multiple drones. In order to complete this monumental task the drones first find a water source using sensors and after it has found a water source, it will use a specifc algorithm to make sure that the water source is aplicable to the closest village while being clean at the same time. The algorithm will determine if the villagers can get to the water source without much difficulty and weather it is drinkable.
